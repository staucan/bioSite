Candace Martin

From South Carolina to Florida

Candace is a young Native American who was born and raised in South Carolina, where her single mother put her through boarding school. She went to boarding school throughout her High School years so that she could have a better education and further her dance career. She started dance ballet at the age of 3, and continued to do so until she was in her young twenties and hurt her ACL. She left South Carolina to pursue a career in dancing with Disney, she started out at a Disney College Program Student and then decided to remain in Florida and have her first business career. She started in food and beverage, then moved into working attractions frontline, then worked her way up to being a coordinator then shortly following being a manager. She decided to leave the Disney Company to further her career with Progressive Insurance and is now a Bodily Injury Claims Adjuster. She has been with this company for almost 2 years because it provides her with an amazing work/life balance.
